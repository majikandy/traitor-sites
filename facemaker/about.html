About Facemaker

Facemaker was started in 2018 by potter Jo Marsh in a small studio in rural Shropshire. What began as an obsession with human expression in clay has grown into a full range of ceramic wall faces, each one thrown, sculpted and glazed entirely by hand.

No two faces are alike. Variations in glaze, texture and expression are part of the charm — and the point. We use locally sourced stoneware, earthenware and porcelain clays, fired in a wood kiln twice a year and a smaller electric kiln for smaller runs.

All faces are suitable for indoor and sheltered outdoor display. Orders are dispatched within 5 working days, packaged in recycled materials.
